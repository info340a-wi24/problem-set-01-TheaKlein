Kiki the Witch

Info about Kiki

Kiki is from Kikis Delivery Service, which is a Studio Ghibli movie. Kiki is a witch, and part of her training includes flying to another city and living there for a year to develop her talent. She has brown hair, brown eyes, and a red bow that she always wears. She also has a black cat named Jiji. This story is about her trying to find herself and figure out her purpose as a witch.

Three reasons I admire Kiki

  • I admire Kiki because she has a strong tenacity despite having many setbacks.
  • Kiki is a very kind person, and she always looks out for others.
  • Kiki’s cat is my favorite character, so by proxy, I have to like Kiki as well.
